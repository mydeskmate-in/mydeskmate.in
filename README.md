## 🌟 About the Project
**MyDeskmate.in** is an all-in-one digital utility platform designed for students, freelancers, and small businesses in India. Instead of paying for expensive software like Adobe, MyDeskmate provides **professional-grade AI tools** for free.

Our focus is on **Local Privacy**: Most of our tools process data locally, ensuring your documents never stay on a server.

*   **🤖 AI Resume Humanizer:** Bypass AI detection (GPTZero, etc.) and naturally rewrite resumes.
*   **📸 AI Passport Photo Maker:** Instantly convert selfies into official government-sized ID photos (Aadhar/PAN ready).
*   **💎 AI Jewelry Enhancer:** Professional gold and diamond photo enhancement for e-commerce sellers.
*   **🛡️ Watermark Remover:** Smart AI detection to remove logos and text from images.
